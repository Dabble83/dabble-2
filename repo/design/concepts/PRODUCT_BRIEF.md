# Dabble — Product Brief

## What Dabble Is
Dabble is a lightweight, peer-to-peer platform for discovering and sharing skills
in real-world settings. It focuses on curiosity, experimentation, and informal learning
rather than professional instruction or certification.

Dabble is about trying something new with people nearby.

---

## Core User Types
- **Dabbler**: someone who wants to try or learn something new
- **Guide**: someone willing to share experience or tips
- Users can be both at the same time

There is no expert hierarchy or credentialing.

---

## Core Use Cases
- A traveler wants to try a local outdoor activity with someone experienced
- A neighbor wants quick help learning a home improvement task
- Someone wants to explore a creative hobby with others nearby

Sessions are short, informal, and optional.

---

## What Dabble Is Not
- Not a professional services marketplace
- Not formal instruction or lessons
- Not a certification or credentialing platform
- Not focused on long-term gigs or contracts

---

## Initial Categories
1. **Adventure & Outdoors**
   - Skiing, snowboarding, hiking, climbing, kayaking, surfing, etc.

2. **Home Improvement & Gardening**
   - DIY basics, repairs, gardening, organization

3. **Creative & Hobbies**
   - Drawing, knitting, woodworking, music, photography, writing

---

## Core Product Flows
1. Explore nearby activities (map-based discovery)
2. Try something new (browse skills by category)
3. Meet other Dabblers and Guides
4. Share what you know

---

## MVP Scope
Included:
- Landing page
- Try Something New map page
- Category skill lists
- Signup with interests and abilities
- Ability self-rating (Beginner → Expert)

Not included (yet):
- Payments
- Messaging
- Reviews
- Certifications
- Public profiles

---

## Product Principles
- Low pressure
- Friendly and human
- Curious, not authoritative
- Community over transactions
